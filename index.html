About Me

I am currently a Post-doc Researcher at the School of Automation Science and Engineering, South China University of Technology, Guangzhou, China. My research interests include computer vision, pattern recognition, and deep learning. My work primarily revolves around biometric recognition, with an emphasis on face and gait recognition.
